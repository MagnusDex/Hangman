How to play Hangman

The computer thinks of a word.

Note: In this particular version the word is always an adjective (like blue, skillful, huge, etc.)

Each letter of the word is marked by an underscore ( _ ).

Your task is to guess the word, letter by letter.

If you guess a letter that is in the word, it will be shown everywhere it appears in the word

If the letter isn't in the word then a gallow with a hanging man will gradually be drawn, step by step for each wrong letter.

You will continue guessing letters until you either solve the word or the picture of the hanging man is completed.

The picture is completed in 8 steps, so you can miss 7 times and still succeed. But if you miss 8 times, you lose.
